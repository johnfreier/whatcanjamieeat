A really filling breakfast.

### Ingredients
- 1 slice of glutten free vegan bread
- 1 Avocado
- 1 egg
- Olive Oil
- Salt
- Pepper

### Instructions
1. Toast Bread
2. Add Olive Oil on bread.
3. Cut out Avocado and Slice on Toast.
4, Fry Egg, over easy.
5. Add egg on top of Avocado.
6. Add salt.
7. Add pepper.

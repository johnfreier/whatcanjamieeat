A yummy desert pudding.

### Ingredients
- 1 cup Chia Seads
- 1 can Coconut Milk
- 1/2 cup Pumpkin Puree
- 4 Tbsp. Maple Syrup
- 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
- 1/2 Tsp Ginger
- 1/2 Tsp Vanilla

### Instructions
Mix all together. Let stand 1/2 hour or so before serving.
